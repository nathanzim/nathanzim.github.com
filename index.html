Nathan Zimmerman's Baseball Simulator


Welcome to my baseball simulator. I have taken data from every at bat over the past 4 seasons (Over 600,000 records) and created a model that reflects a player's probability of specific outcomes. (Walks, Singles, Groundouts, etc.) What makes the model powerful is that it adjusts for level of competition, so that each player can be analyzed independently.

The simulator has a ton of potential. You can simulate thousands of hypothetical games in just seconds, with the lineups and pitchers of your choosing. Ever wondered how moving a player from third to fourth in the batting order will affect a teams performance? Want to measure the benefit of a potential trade? The simulator is also perfect for predicting upcoming games, as it provides a predicted run total and win probability. The simulator is a free web app that you can now access through the link below. I refit the model with new data weekly, and I am actively improving the functionality of the simulator.
